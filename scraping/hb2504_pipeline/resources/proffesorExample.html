Hettie C. Houghton

Senior Lecturer
Accounting and Information Systems
Office Building: Business Administration Building
Office Room: Rm. 256
Phone: (915) 747-7734
Email: hchoughton@utep.edu
Bio

Hettie Houghton is a lecturer in the Accounting Department. She worked in public accounting doing governmental and not-for-profit auditing prior to joining the accounting faculty in 2009. Her primary areas of teaching are financial principles of accounting, managerial accounting, cost and tax accounting. Hettie is a member of the Institute of Management Accountants and is faculty advisor for the Accounting Society and Beta Alpha Psi.


Awards and Honors
  • Teaching Excellence award for Teaching Innovation (04/01/2022-04/30/2022)
  • Teaching Excellence Award for Student Intellectual and Career Development (04/01/2021-04/30/2021)
  • Teaching Excellence Award College of Business May 2019 - COBA (05/10/2019)
  • Teaching Excellence Award College of Business 2017 (04/21/2017)
  • Nominated as Educator of the Year Texas Society of CPA's 2015 - Texas Society of CPA's (05/31/2015)
  • Teaching Excellence Award 2015 - College of Business Administration (04/17/2015)
  • Excellence in Undergraduate Instruction Accounting - Business College Council (04/12/2013)
  • Top Ten Senior Award - University of Texas at El Paso (01/01/2006-12/31/2006)
  • Woman of UTEP - University of Texas at El Paso (01/01/2006-12/31/2006)
Education
  • MAcc in Accounting, University of Texas at El Paso (2007)
  • BBA in Accounting, University of Texas at El Paso (2005)
